D23: The Ultimate Disney Fan Event

Project Management & Web Production


For D23: The Ultimate Disney Fan Event, I worked cross-functionally with UX designers, marketing, editorial, and legal teams to manage and implement multiple pages, including the splash page, Disney Legends pages, and Sponsors page. I managed priorities across each team and coordinated asset delivery to meet a firm launch deadline. Because the D23 Expo and Disney Legends announcements drove high engagement across social media, QA was crucial to handle the resulting volume of traffic to these pages.
