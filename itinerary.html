MyItinerary Application

The MyItinerary application is a website where users can keep all their trips in one place and plan itinerary items for each trip. It also features travel-related content, including articles and travel ideas, on the homepage.

Funtionality: Users can create a destination by entering the start and end dates, along with a custom picture. Once a trip is created, all the dates within that trip are displayed on the page, allowing users to add itinerary items. Each itinerary item includes details such as the date, time, links, location, and category (e.g., activity, food, travel, etc.). Itinerary items are color-coded based on their category, and clicking on an item reveals more details about the experience.
