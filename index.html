About Me

Hi, I'm Ella!

I am a digital producer and UX designer, working in the intersection of technology and design to deliver quality user experiences. I most recently worked as a UX Designer and Digital Producer for The Walt Disney Company, where I led end-to-end production on high-traffic web and mobile initiatives. This role required flexibility to take on either a project management or design position, depending on each project's needs.

I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Web Design and Development with an emphasis in UX Design. I also have experience with graphic design and digital marketing. My background has shaped me into a user-focused designer who always prioritizes accessibility and responsiveness.
