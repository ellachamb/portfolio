Discover St. George Campaign

St. George, Utah is a growing community in Southern Utah with gorgeous red rock formations and countless outdoor offerings. With a few national parks nearby, it has become a popular tourist destination with millions visiting every year. While living here I was inspired by the beautiful scenery and wanted to incorporate it into a design project. This led me to create a mock campaign to promote tourism for the city, highlighting the many different outdoor activities that visitors and residents can experience.
